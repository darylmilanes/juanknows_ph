Consumer Rights

"NO RETURN,
NO EXCHANGE"

Is this policy legal?

NO.

According to the
Consumer Act of the Philippines (RA 7394)

If an item is defective, stores MUST offer a replacement, repair, or refund. They cannot post "No Return" signs.
